<p>Jeremy introduces Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) with an intuitive comparison to art counterfeiting, along with some of its interesting applications such as Style Transfer. He also presents some variations on plain GANs that are adapted for other tasks. Yann LeCun, Facebook's AI research director said that adversarial networks are “the most interesting idea in the last 10 years in ML.” They are a very important model architecture and should be understood thoroughly.</p>
