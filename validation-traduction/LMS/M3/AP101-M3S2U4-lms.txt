<p>Jeremy overviews some classic open-source datasets used to benchmark a CNN architecture’s performance in various tasks, such as MNIST, fashion MNIST, SVHN, ImageNet and COCO. He also presents some important tips to keep in mind when collecting your own data for machine learning tasks. The information in this video is useful to any and all who plan on using CNNs, whether it be to benchmark model performance or run your own data through a CNN.</p>
