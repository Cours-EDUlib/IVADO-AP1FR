<p>The same year as VGG, GoogLeNet was published. Jeremy presents the model’s architecture in an approachable, modular way. He overviews the inception module - their main contribution to CNN literature. He also explains how GoogLeNet managed to get more depth with fewer parameters, a substantial improvement over previous networks. </p>
