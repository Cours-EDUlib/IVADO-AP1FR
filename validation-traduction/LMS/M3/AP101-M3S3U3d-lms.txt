<p>Jeremy introduces ResNet, the first CNN model to beat human performance on top-5 accuracy in the ILSVRC competition. He presents the information bottleneck associated with too much depth and how the authors of ResNet overcome this challenge using skip connections. Jeremy also overviews Batch Normalization, a form of regularization used in ResNet. It is a useful trick to normalize feature maps of a network using batch statistics and learning and helps regularize learning.</p>
