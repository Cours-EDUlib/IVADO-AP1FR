In this first task, you will reproduce an experiment of the seminal paper Long Short-Term Memory by Sepp Hochreiter and Jürgen Schmidhuber. This experiment shows that LSTMs can typically handle longer dependencies than vanilla RNNs on a toy task. For the second task, you will train an RNN-based language model by using word embeddings. Finally, you will evaluate the model qualitatively by generating some text.</p>
