<p>Mirko goes over the link between words and semantics, as well as some key difficulties that arise when reconciling these two elements of natural language. He presents two key concepts that help with this reconciliation - the Distributional Hypothesis and the Principle of Compositionality. The content in this video is paramount to understanding the difficulties that NLP practitioners encounter when processing natural language, and the key concepts behind the solutions to these problems.</p>
