<p>Mirko introduces various classical approaches to represent text in NLP. He compares three strategies (words/subwords/characters) from the point of view of the size of vocabulary, sentence length, and handling of out-of-vocabulary tokens. Mirko also presents the one-hot representation for tokens.</p>
