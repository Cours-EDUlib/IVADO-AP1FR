<p>Mirko summarizes the NLP Section and emphasizes the key element which links almost all NLP tasks - the need to link words and semantics. He also explains the key element that is common to all state of the art NLP models: unsupervised pre-training allows models to train on large quantities of data, even if the labelled data for the task of interest is scarce.</p>
