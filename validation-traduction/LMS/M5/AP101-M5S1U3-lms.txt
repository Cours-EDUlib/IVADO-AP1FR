<p>Golnoosh introduces mathematical definitions of fairness. For group fairness, she contrasts demographic parity, equal opportunity and equalized odds - three different mathematical definitions of a type of fairness. For individual fairness, she explains why blind fairness is ineffective. She also introduces mathematical concepts to define causal discrimination and the idea of fairness through awareness.</p>
