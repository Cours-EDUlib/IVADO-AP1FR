<p>Golnoosh introduces fairness in postprocessing by explaining how black boxes can be post-processed to ensure fairness. She also introduces the idea of fairness verification, which is an active area of research centered on finding a shortlist of criteria to verify that would guarantee a model’s fairness. She walks the viewer through an example of fairness verification by introducing Deep Verification Networks.</p>
