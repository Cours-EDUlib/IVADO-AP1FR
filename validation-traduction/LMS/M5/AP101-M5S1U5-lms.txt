<p>Golnoosh concludes the module on bias and discrimination by detailing the various challenges in the field, such as the cost of fairness constraints on model performance, variations in the definition of fairness across fields and the lack of clarity in data communication channels. She also presents opportunities such as leveraging the complexity of the real world to include fairness in models and addressing fairness in subgroups that can be non-binary. She closes her talk with a few key takeaways.</p>
